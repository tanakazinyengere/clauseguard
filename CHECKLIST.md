# The 10-Point Contract Checklist for Freelancers & SMBs
*Don't sign until you check these.*

1. **IP Ownership:** Does the contract state "Work for Hire"? If so, you own nothing. Ensure it says ownership transfers *only* upon full payment.
2. **Payment Terms:** Watch for "Net-90" or "Pay-when-paid". Negotiate for Net-15 or Net-30.
3. **Termination for Convenience:** Can they fire you tomorrow without paying for work done? Ensure there's a 14-day notice period.
4. **Indemnification Traps:** Are you promising to pay their legal fees if *they* get sued? Limit your liability to the amount of the contract.
5. **Scope Creep Protection:** Is the "Statement of Work" specific? If it's vague, you'll be doing extra work for free.
6. **Acceptance Window:** How long do they have to "approve" work? Limit it to 5 business days, after which it's "deemed accepted."
7. **Late Fees:** Do you have a 5-10% late fee clause? Without it, you are an interest-free loan.
8. **Jurisdiction:** If there's a dispute, do you have to fly to another country to go to court? Keep it local.
9. **Exclusivity:** Are they banning you from working with other clients? Unless they pay a massive premium, say no.
10. **Non-Solicitation:** Can you hire their people? Can they hire yours? Make it mutual.
