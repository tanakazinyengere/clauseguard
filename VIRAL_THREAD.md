Here are the 5 "Red Flags" that are legally robbing you right now (and how to fix them): 🧵

2/5 THE PORTFOLIO KILLER: "Work for Hire." 
If you see this, you own nothing. Not even the right to show the work in your portfolio. 
FIX: Change to "Transfer of ownership upon full payment."

3/5 THE INTEREST-FREE LOAN: "Net-90 Payment."
Clients use you as a bank. You finish work in Jan, you get paid in April. 
FIX: Demand "Net-15" or "Net-30" with a 5% late fee per month.

4/5 THE LEGAL BLACK HOLE: "Broad Indemnification."
They want you to pay THEIR legal fees if they get sued for something unrelated. 
FIX: Limit your liability to the total fee of the project.

5/5 THE SCOPE CREEP TRAP: "Vague Acceptance."
If they don't have a deadline to approve work, they can ask for 'one more tweak' for 6 months.
FIX: "Work is deemed accepted if no feedback is received within 5 business days."
